Group Mediclaim Policy - 2018-19

You and your nuclear family (Definition of nuclear family : Spouse + max. of 2 children and dependent parents/parents in Law) are covered up to the opted insurance per year on a family floater basis. You need to enrol your immediate family (Spouse & 2 Children and dependent parents/parents in law) on or before the 3rd of the subsequent month of joining if you wish to do so. Medical Insurance cover for you will be effected by default from the date of your Date of Joining. However, you MUST enrol your dependents by submission of details on or before the 3rd of the subsequent month of joining. Employees have an option to include either their Parents or Parents-in-Law, to be covered in pairs and mid-year inclusion of parents post the enrolment window period is not allowed. Mid-term inclusion of only new born babies (within 45 days of the child birth) and newly married spouse (within 45 days from the date of marriage) is allowed. The insurance coverage for the newly acquired dependent (spouse/child) will be with effect from the date of event (marriage/ birth whichever is applicable), subject to enrolling the new dependent within 45 days from the date of event. Feel free to raise a query on Unified Helpdesk for clarifications, if any.
