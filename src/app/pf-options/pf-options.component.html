Altimetrik INDIA | Provident Fund Options Declaration | FY 2019-20

Employees Provident Fund Act 1952 allowes to contribute to your Provident Fund Account either of the follwing two:

OPTION I. 12% of your Basic pay per month ( For Example if Basic Pay is INR 50000 per month, contribution to Your EPFO account would be INR 12000 per month; INR 6000 as employees contribution and another INR 6000 as employers Contribution)

OPTION II. 12% of notional basic of INR 15000 per month, i.e INR 1800 per month as employees contribution and another INR 1800 per month as employer contribution. ( The differential amount of INR 4200 is added with FBP component as taxable salary to keep the CTC per month as Fixed.

Option II would ensure restricting or reducing the contribution to PF to increase take home pay as a result however it also means increased Income tax liability and reduction in corpus you en cash at your retirement.

This form is designed to receive your choices between the two options. Kindly note the following before deciding and exercising your PF option:

1. Your CTC remain constant irrespective of your choice made, and the differential salary is adjusted with your FBP (flexible Benefit Pay) component in your salary.

2. This option is static, and not available for edit again, due to statutory restrictions, thus your careful deliberation is requested before you make your choice.
